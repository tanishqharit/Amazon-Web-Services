# S3 Glacier Flexible Retrieval

## What is it?

**S3 Glacier Flexible Retrieval** (formerly just "S3 Glacier") is for **archive data** where you can wait **minutes to hours** to get it back.

---

## Retrieval Options

| Option | Time | Cost |
|--------|------|------|
| **Expedited** | 1-5 minutes | Most expensive |
| **Standard** | 3-5 hours | Medium |
| **Bulk** | 5-12 hours | Cheapest |

---

## Key Features

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Access** | Not instant — minutes to hours |
| **Storage cost** | Very low |
| **Min storage duration** | 90 days |
| **Best for** | Backups, archives where delay is OK |

---

## When to Use

- Backup data you hope to never need
- Old log files for compliance
- Historical data for annual reviews
- When cost matters more than speed

---

## Key Takeaway

> Glacier Flexible Retrieval is very cheap archive storage. Retrieval takes minutes to hours depending on the option you choose. 90-day minimum storage.
